1. How can I send emotes from my pet?

/pet wants to eat a gnome.

Character: Funny
Pet: Mad (Bear)

PetEmote users see:
Mad wants to eat a gnome.
Others see:
Funny 's Bear Mad wants to eat a gnome.

2. How can I change the pet family for others than PetEmote users?

/pet family fat bear
PetEmote users see:
Mad wants to eat a gnome.
Others see:
Funny 's fat bear Mad wants to eat a gnome.

3. How can I reset the pet family to its original value?

/pet family reset

4. Version History

PetEmote 1.0 - 2006/07/19

A simple command to add your pet's family and name to your emotes.

PetEmote 1.1 - 2006/07/27

Added some core functions, so that you can see emotes really coming from your pet.

PetEmote 1.2 - 2006/07/29

Added a command to change the pet family that can be seen by other players.
